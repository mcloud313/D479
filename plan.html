Transportation

Getting to Taniti

Taniti can be reached by air via a small airport that accommodates jets and propeller planes. Cruise ships also dock in Yellow Leaf Bay once a week.

Getting Around Taniti

Public buses serve Taniti City. Taxis and rental cars are available. Biking is a popular option, especially in flat areas like Merriton Landing.
